I am a postdoctoral scholar in AI-EDGE Institute at The Ohio State University (OSU) working with Prof. Ness Shroff. I graduated with a Ph.D. from University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). My advisor was Prof. Ting Zhu.

Prior to joining UMBC, I worked in Chinese Academy of Sciences as an electro-optical engineer. Before that, I received my M.S. and B.S. from Beijing Institute of Technology.
